Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is usually made with various add-ons like nuts or chocolate chips. This is a base recipe that can be expanded and experimented upon.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Beat sugar and butter together until smooth.
  3. Add eggs and mashed bananas to the butter mixture until well-combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Stir the two mixtures together.
  6. Bake for around 60 minutes. Check with a toothpick — it should come out clean.
  7. Let the bread cool.
